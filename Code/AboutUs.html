ABOUT US



Bursary Bank was founded in 2021 by a group of students at Cape Peninsula University of Technology (CPUT). As students we have personally looked for bursaries at one point and most websites you initially find are over complicated or outdated, especially for new students who are not familiar with the process. We understand the struggle of finding funding for studies, which is why we developed a way of simplifying the bursary search process for our students. We have created a website that is catered towards helping CPUT students acquire alternative forms of funding. The website showcases bursary opportunities that apply to our students.
